Runci

It's with great pleasure to introduce you to the best block on the university campus. Over the years, Block R.U.N.C.I has excelled and strives to remain on the road to greatness. We have managed to maintain and improve our strong and reputable image on hall and on the university scene by continuing to excel in sports, culture, education and extracurricular activities and as such, we continue on our road to lifelong success. We have created great history over the years as a block and as such we take pride in acknowledging all of our super seniors who have played a part in the growth of the block and the building of its traditions over the years. Some of who have taken up prestigious roles in our society. One of which we are proud to highlight is the Most Honourable Andrew Holness, leader of the Jamaica Labour Party and Prime Minister of Jamaica, who resided on the block and visit from time to time to participate in hall events. It is no secret that we are characterized by UNITY and STRENGTH as we aim to center all of our achievements on these. Over the years, we have managed to secure the Leslie Robinson trophy for two consecutive years, winning sports day 13 consecutive years and 10 time inter-block football champions as would be represented by the number of stars on our block jersey. While we dedicate ourselves to thrive in culture and sports, we make it our duty to help with the overall development of each member of the block. With aspects such as education, health and proper social ethics as our top priority as a family, it should come as no surprise that we have the highest collective GPA as a block. Each year Block R.U.N.C.I welcomes a fresh batch of first years to offer the creative minds and talents to the block and hall and be inspired by us and find out who you are and where you want to go and never settle for mediocrity.
